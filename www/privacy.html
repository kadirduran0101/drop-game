Privacy Policy — DROP

Effective: 2026-05-02

What we collect

DROP runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store personal information.

  • No accounts — no name, email, phone, or address.
  • No location — no GPS, no IP-based geolocation.
  • No advertising IDs — no IDFA, no GAID, no fingerprinting.
  • No third-party analytics — no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no equivalent.

What's stored locally on your device

The game uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Your personal-best score and total play count.
  • Your settings (mute on/off).
  • An anonymous session ID generated each visit, used only for in-browser ringbuffer telemetry that never leaves your device.

You can clear this anytime by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Cookies

We do not set any cookies.

Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

The game is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no personal data, COPPA and GDPR-K consent flows are not applicable.

EU / UK / Türkiye users

GDPR / KVKK: we have no data to give you, delete, or port. There is nothing to consent to.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add telemetry that leaves your device (e.g. anonymous server beacon), we will update this policy with a clear effective date and changelog.

Contact

Questions: open an issue at the project repository.
